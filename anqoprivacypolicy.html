Privacy Policy

Anqo — by Ian Spresney

Last updated: May 2026
01

Overview

Anqo does not collect personal data. Your flashcards, decks, and study data are stored on-device and synced privately via iCloud. Ads are served by Google AdMob.

This Privacy Policy explains how Anqo ("the app") handles your information. By using Anqo, you agree to the practices described here.

02

On-Device Processing

The following features are processed entirely on your device and do not transmit data to Ian Spresney or any external server:

  • All flashcard and deck creation, editing, and review
  • AI-powered import and quiz generation via Apple Intelligence
  • Importing from Anki (.apkg), Quizlet, and other sources
  • Study statistics and streak tracking
03

iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

Anqo uses Apple CloudKit to sync your decks, cards, and study progress across your Apple devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to Ian Spresney.

iCloud sync is subject to Apple's privacy policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy. You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device's Settings.

04

Apple Intelligence & AI Features

AI-powered features in Anqo — including note import and quiz generation — use Apple Intelligence and run entirely on-device. No content you provide is sent to Ian Spresney or any third-party AI service.

Apple Intelligence requires a compatible device and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

05

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Anqo uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve ads, including device identifiers and usage data, in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

You can review Google's data practices at policies.google.com/privacy. Users in applicable regions may be shown a consent prompt for personalized ads as required by law.

Upgrading to a premium subscription removes all ads.

06

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. Ian Spresney does not receive or store your payment information. Purchase records are managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

07

Children's Privacy

Anqo does not knowingly collect personal data from any user. Note that Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers as described in section 05. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, you can disable personalized ads in your device's Privacy settings.

08

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in a material way, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the support page.
